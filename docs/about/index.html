About us

The team of highly-qualified specialists is here to provide your pets with the best medical,surgical and preventive care possible.

Outstanding customer service, personal care to each and every problem of our patients, safe and pleasant atmosphere.

The clinic has an x-ray imaging room, cardiology room, prep room, laboratory, operating theatre, equipped with the latest medical equipment, such as an eye surgery machine, surgical microscope, ultrasound, x-ray, oxygen supply machine and a lot more.

On the premises of SoftClinic, there is also a post-surgery rehabilitation sector and a critical care sector.

By creating a stress-free environment and keeping the highest level of services, we aim to provide our customers with the bes possible care.

An international certificate confirming the results is issued to every animal tested for such diseases in our clinic.
